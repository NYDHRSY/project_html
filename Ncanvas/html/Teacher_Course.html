Your attention is required

We hope these information could help you editing.

  1. Our system requires that you must complete all input to the current form before you start a new course editing or adding a new course.
  2. Our system requires that you are not allowed to modify the course name and course id when you are editing the course.
  3. Our system requires that when you are editing a course or adding a course, the days are represented by numbers (1: Mon, 2: Tue, etc.), separated by commas.
  4. Our system requires that when you are editing a course or adding a course, the cover must be a picture, and the related course introductions are the markdown files.
  5. Our system requires that when you are editing a course or adding a course, all inputs should avoid contains the symbols which Mysql does not support (like ' & etc.), or the action will fail.
